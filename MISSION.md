# Mission: Poker Competition Readiness + AI Coach Builder

## Why
I am learning poker to progress from complete beginner to competition-capable player as a long-term hobby. In parallel, I will build and use a Python/AI backend with a React frontend as my personal coaching system so my training stays structured, measurable, and adaptive.

## Success looks like
- I complete a structured daily training loop (30 minutes study, 30 minutes play) consistently.
- I become decision-disciplined at online 6-max No-Limit Hold'em micro-stakes before scaling complexity.
- I transition from cash-first fundamentals to tournament-focused preparation after the foundation phase.
- I ship and actively use a personal poker coach app that tracks hands, scores decision quality, and assigns targeted drills.

## Constraints
- Time budget: 60 minutes per day total (30 study, 30 play).
- Baseline: complete beginner; currently knows hand rankings only.
- Training preference: interactive practice plus printable/useful cheatsheets.
- Foundation protocol: first 14 days are play-money only to optimize deliberate practice and decision quality.
- Session structure during foundation: one-table play only for cleaner decision reps and lower cognitive load.
- Risk policy: strict bankroll rules (100 buy-ins at current stake, move down at 80, stop-loss at 3 buy-ins/day).

## Out of scope
- Skipping fundamentals to chase advanced solver-heavy lines too early.
- Aggressive stake jumps during beginner and early intermediate phases.
- Building broad social/community app features before a strong single-user coaching loop exists.
