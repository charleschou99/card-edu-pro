Lesson 0002: Day 1 Foundation Routine

Estimated time: 60 minutes · Format: 30 study + 30 play (one table, play-money)

Mission tie-in: Competition-level growth starts with repeatable routines. Day 1 is about process quality, not winning chips.

Block A (30 min study)

  1. 8 min: Review position order and say it out loud 3 times.
  2. 10 min: Review RFI baseline; focus only on UTG/HJ vs BTN differences.
  3. 7 min: Pot-odds quick table drill: recall 1/2 pot = 25%, pot-size = 33%.
  4. 5 min: Pre-session commitment:
    • I announce position before acting.
    • I fold uncertain early-position hands.
    • I tag 3 confusing hands.

Block B (30 min play)

  • Play one table only.
  • Use play-money only (foundation protocol).
  • Before each preflop action, state your position quietly.
  • If decision feels close in early position, default to fold and note it.
  • Capture 3 spots where you felt uncertain (screenshot or short note).

5-minute review (after play)

  1. How many hands did I play out of position that I now think were too loose?
  2. Did I follow one-table rule for full 30 minutes?
  3. Did I tag exactly 3 uncertain hands for analysis?

Score yourself from 0-2 for each item. Day-1 target: 4+/6 process score.
