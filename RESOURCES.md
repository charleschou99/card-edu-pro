# Poker Competition + AI Coach Resources

## Knowledge

- [PokerStars Learn: Pot Odds](https://www.pokerstars.com/poker/learn/lesson/pot-odds/)
  Clear foundation for call/fold math, including common bet-size equity thresholds and the Rule of 4 and 2. Use for: daily pot-odds drills and early postflop decision checks.
- [PokerCoaching: Free Preflop Charts](https://pokercoaching.com/preflop-charts/)
  Position-based preflop baseline from a high-volume tournament professional. Use for: beginner RFI decisions and range-discipline training.
- [PokerCoaching: Free Poker Cheat Sheets](https://pokercoaching.com/cheatsheets/)
  Quick printable references for position, sizing, and preflop habits. Use for: pre-session warmup and reducing in-game decision friction.
- [PokerCoaching 100BB Preflop PDF](https://poker-coaching.s3.amazonaws.com/tools/preflop-charts/full-preflop-charts.pdf)
  Concrete chart pack for raise-first-in and responses vs raises/3-bets. Use for: focused preflop memorization and edge-hand drills.
- [Pokerology: Starting Hand Selection](https://www.pokerology.com/poker/strategy/starting-hand-selection/)
  Beginner-friendly explanation of position and tight-to-wide range expansion. Use for: conceptual understanding before detailed chart memorization.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [Two Plus Two Poker Forums](https://forumserver.twoplustwo.com/)
  Long-running high-signal discussion board with deep hand-analysis archives. Use for: posting hand histories and getting line critiques.
- [r/poker](https://www.reddit.com/r/poker/)
  Large community with varied skill levels and frequent practical discussion. Use for: trend awareness, tool suggestions, and light hand-review feedback.
- Local poker meetup or card-room low-stakes community
  Real-table behavioral and timing reads are hard to simulate fully online. Use for: live adaptation reps and emotional-control practice.

## Gaps

- A single, stable source of legal online-room recommendations by region and compliance status.
- A trusted, beginner-friendly tournament transition curriculum mapped week-by-week after cash fundamentals.
