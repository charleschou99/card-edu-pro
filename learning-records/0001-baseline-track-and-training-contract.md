# Baseline, Track, and Training Contract Established

The learner is a complete beginner in poker (hand rankings only) with strong math comfort and enough coding skill to build a personal training app. We fixed an initial coaching path: online 6-max No-Limit Hold'em micro-stakes, decision-quality-first evaluation, strict bankroll discipline, and a later transition to tournament prep after fundamentals are stable. This sets the zone of proximal development for the first phase to preflop position discipline, hand selection, and basic pot-odds decisions.
